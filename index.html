About

Paperfox is mainly the home of Nathalie Garfinkle's evolving Tarot app. As a developer and designer professionally, and an artist by passion she finds it's very rewarding to work on something that makes use of such a wide range of her skills. If she's not working on adding something new to this, you'll likely find her doodling up some of the card images, playing with her dog, or out in her garden.
